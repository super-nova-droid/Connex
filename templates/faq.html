Frequently Asked Questions

What is Connex? ▼
Connex is a digital wellness platform designed to connect seniors with volunteers for various events and activities, fostering a supportive community focused on digital literacy and wellness.
How do I register for an account? ▼
Click on the 'Sign Up' button in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form. You'll need to verify your email address with an OTP code before your account is activated.
What types of events are available? ▼
We offer various events including digital literacy workshops, fitness classes, health seminars, social gatherings, arts and crafts sessions, and volunteer opportunities.
How can I volunteer? ▼
When registering, select the volunteer option. Once your account is verified, you can browse events and sign up to volunteer. You can also volunteer for specific events you're attending.
How do I sign up for events? ▼
Browse available events on the Events page, click on an event you're interested in, and click the "Sign Up for Event" button. You can view your signed-up events on your calendar.
Can I cancel my event registration? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your registration by going to the event details page and clicking "Remove Sign-up" or by managing your events through your calendar.
How do I contact support? ▼
You can contact support by visiting the Support page and submitting a ticket, or by using our AI chatbot for immediate assistance with common questions.
Is there a mobile app? ▼
Currently, Connex is available as a web application that works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future release.
