Frequently Asked Questions

What is Connex? ►
Connex is a digital wellness platform designed to connect seniors with volunteers for various events and activities, fostering a supportive and engaging community. We aim to bridge the digital divide and enhance the well-being of seniors through technology.
How do I register for an account? ►
To register, click on the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar. You will be guided through a simple process to create your account. You'll need a valid email address and to create a secure password.
What kind of events are available on Connex? ►
We offer a wide range of events tailored for seniors, including digital literacy workshops (e.g., smartphone basics, online safety), social gatherings, light fitness classes (e.g., chair yoga), arts & crafts sessions, storytelling, and more. Check our "Events" page for the full list!
How can I sign up to volunteer? ►
If you're interested in becoming a volunteer, you can find more information and sign-up details on our "Volunteer" section. We welcome individuals passionate about helping seniors engage with technology and community activities.
Is Connex free to use? ►
Yes, Connex is completely free for seniors to join and participate in events. Our mission is to provide accessible digital wellness resources to the community.
How do I contact support if I have an issue? ►
For any questions or support, please visit our "Support" page where you can find contact information and common troubleshooting tips. You can also reach out to us via email at support@connex.com.
What is digital wellness for seniors? ►
Digital wellness for seniors focuses on empowering older adults to confidently and safely navigate the digital world. This includes topics like understanding online privacy, recognizing scams, using communication apps, and leveraging technology for health and social connection.
