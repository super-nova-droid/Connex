Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Connex, digital wellness events, and how to get the most out of our platform.

Getting Started

What is Connex and how does it work? +
Connex is a digital wellness platform designed specifically for seniors. We help you discover and participate in technology-focused events, workshops, and activities in your community. Our platform makes it easy to find events near you, sign up, and connect with others who share your interests in learning about technology.
How do I register for an account? +
Creating an account is simple:
  • Click "Sign Up" on our homepage
  • Fill in your basic information
  • Choose your preferred community center
  • Verify your email (optional) or set up security questions
  • Start exploring events!
Is there a mobile app? +
Currently, Connex is available as a web application that works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future release.

Events & Activities

What types of events are available? +
We offer various events including digital literacy workshops, fitness classes, health seminars, social gatherings, arts and crafts sessions, and volunteer opportunities.
How do I find events near me? +
When you sign up, we'll automatically detect your location and show you events at nearby community centers. You can also use our location selector to choose your preferred community center. All events are displayed with clear dates, times, and locations.
How do I sign up for events? +
Browse available events on the Events page, click on an event you're interested in, and click the "Sign Up for Event" button. You can view your signed-up events on your calendar.
Can I cancel my event registration? +
Yes, you can cancel your registration by going to the event details page and clicking "Remove Sign-up" or by managing your events through your calendar.

Account Management

How do I create an account? +
Creating an account is simple:
  • Click "Sign Up" on our homepage
  • Fill in your basic information
  • Choose your preferred community center
  • Verify your email (optional) or set up security questions
  • Start exploring events!
How do I reset my password? +
To reset your password:
  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password
How do I update my profile information? +
To update your profile information:
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Profile" section
  3. Update your information and preferences
  4. Save changes to update your profile

Technical Support

What devices can I use to access Connex? +
Connex works on any device with an internet browser:
  • Desktop computers (Windows, Mac)
  • Laptops and tablets
  • Smartphones (iPhone, Android)
  • Smart TVs with browser capability
We've designed our platform to be senior-friendly with large text and easy navigation.
How do I report a technical issue? +
To report a technical issue:
  1. Visit the Support page
  2. Fill out the technical issue form
  3. Provide as much detail as possible
  4. Submit the form and check your email for updates
Is there a user manual or guide for Connex? +
Yes, we provide a user manual and guide:
  • A comprehensive user manual is available on our website
  • In-app guides and tooltips are provided during your first login
  • Video tutorials are available on our YouTube channel

Safety & Privacy

Is my personal information safe? +
Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously:
  • All data is encrypted and securely stored
  • We never share your information with third parties
  • You can delete your account at any time
  • Optional email signup means less personal data required
How does Connex ensure my safety during events? +
Your safety is our priority:
  • All events are vetted and approved by our team
  • Volunteer backgrounds are checked for safety
  • Emergency contact information is collected for all participants
  • Safety guidelines are provided for each event
Can I participate in events anonymously? +
Yes, you can choose to participate anonymously:
  • Use a nickname or initials instead of your full name
  • Do not share personal contact information publicly
  • Join events as a guest without creating an account
